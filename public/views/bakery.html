Big. Fresh. Cookies.

Bake enormous cookies and deliver them fresh to Emory University students late at night.

The business

James assembled his founding team of fellow chefs, legal ninjas, and number-crunchers to launch Butterhub Bakery early 2014.

The wow

Butterhub Bakery delivered over 2000 orders of freshly baked cookies to Emory University students while operational.

The publicity

Team Butterhub joined the ranks of Atlanta’s entrepreneurs, placing as finalist in the Siperstein and featured in Her Campus Magazine, Emory Wheel newspaper, Emory Entrepreneurship & Venture Management’s Startup Crunch, Hackathons, Residence Hall Advising, Dr. Cengiz’s Engineering Practicum, and the Emory Scholars Program.

BFC

The Classic. Choco Chip. It has many names and shapes, but remains the most famous cookie of them all. Ours is crisp on the edges and chewy on the inside. It’s also huge. We call it the Big F-------- Cookie.

Big Chocolate

Chocolate. c.1600, from Nahuatl (Aztecan) xocolatl, possibly from xocolia "to make bitter" + atl "water." We’re ok with all that--we just made a bigger version. Chocolate cookie with chocolate chips.

Raisin Oatmeal

Nutterhub

Nut cookies are oddballs; no one agrees on the best way to make them so we see all sorts of crazy--flourless, eggless, butterless (god forbid), or crunchy ones. After weeks of tests and force-feeding our roommates the prototypes, we’ve created a soft, sophisticated peanut cookie.
